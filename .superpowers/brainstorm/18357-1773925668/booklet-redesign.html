Braised Short Ribs
45 min Gluten-Free View Original →

This became our Christmas Eve tradition after Mom found it in 2019. We always double the wine and add a splash of balsamic — Dad's rule. The whole house smells incredible for hours.

Ingredients
3 lbs bone-in short ribs
2 tbsp olive oil
2 onions, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups red wine
2 cups beef broth
3 tbsp tomato paste
2 sprigs rosemary
Salt & pepper
Instructions
01 Season ribs generously with salt and pepper. Sear in hot Dutch oven until deeply browned on all sides.
02 Remove ribs. Sauté onions and garlic until softened. Add tomato paste, cook 2 minutes.
03 Deglaze with wine, scraping up fond. Add broth, rosemary, and return ribs. Cover and braise at 325°F for 3 hours.
Substitutions
Red wine → Beef broth + 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
Short ribs → Chuck roast, cut into 3" pieces
Rosemary → Thyme or bay leaves
Storage
Refrigerate up to 4 days. Flavors improve overnight. Freezes well for 3 months — reheat low and slow in a covered dish at 300°F.
